About Us

Our Story

DB Speed Auto is an independent workshop in Geelong focused on precision diagnostics, performance and long‑term reliability. We bring more than 20 years of hands‑on experience working with Australian, American and European platforms.

Jeep/FCA Specialist

Dealer‑trained on Jeep & FCA systems. From Pentastar and Hemi V8 to diesel platforms, we use factory‑level tools and procedures to fix issues correctly the first time.
